I'm a final year dual degree (bachelors and masters) student at Indian Institute Of Technology, Kharagpur, majoring in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering with a specialization in Vision. I'm also minoring in Computer Science. Currently I am working on vision-based RL with Prof. Saumik Bhattacharya for my master's thesis.

Previously, I was a reseach intern at CLAIRE, EPFL, where I worked on multi-objective RL under Prof. Caglar Gulcehre. I was also a reseach intern at FRPG where I worked on multi-task adaptive RL under Prof. Aamir Ahmad. I was also fortunate to work with Prof. Shishir Kolathaya, RBCCPS IISc, on barrier funstions inspired reward shaping.

At IIT Kharagpur I've worked on NLP and formal language with Prof. Aritra Hazra, and on defense against SSL backdoor attacks with Prof. Somesh Kumar for my bachelor's thesis.
